About Me

Ifedolapo Otiko

I enjoy creating and recreating Nigerian recipes using readily available ingredients. This is just a collection of some of the recipes that I have followed online, learned from my mum, and some that I came up with. I also love to create other indigenous recipes with Nigerian ingredients; it just seems fun to me! To keep up with these recipes and more, you can check out my food blog and follow my social media pages. When I am not creating content for my blog, I am coding! Which is another form of creating something unique.
