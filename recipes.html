Tortilla Wrap with Chicken and Veggies

Ingredients

Method

  • 1 medium or large Tortilla Wrap
  • Shredded Green Cabbage
  • Shredded Carrot
  • Shredded Purple Cabbage
  • Ketchup
  • Maiyonnaise
  • Black Pepper
  • Crushed Pepper Flakes
  • Shredded Chicken or Sliced Chicken Hot dog

Step 1
Mix your maiyonnaise, ketchup, black pepper and crushed pepper flakes in a small bowl.

Step 2
Spread the maiyonnaise mix on the tortilla wrap, Add your chicken, followed by the shredded vegetables. Add more maiyonnaise mix as you desire and wrap it up.

Step 3
Put on a panini press if you desire a crunchy wrap. Serve and enjoy.

Quinoa and Avocado Salad

Ingredients

Method

  • 1 cup cooked Quinoa
  • 1/4 cup diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1/3 shredded chicken
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Step 1
Combine the first 4 ingredients together in a Salad bowl.

Step 2
Add the lemon juice, salt and black pepper to taste.
